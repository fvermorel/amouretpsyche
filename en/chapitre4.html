Chapter 4
Wandering Psyche
The fall
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
Psyche held on to Eros who took her high in the air. But he extricated himself from her and let her drop to the earth. Zephyrus accompanied her in her fall so she would not get hurt. From high in the sky, Eros told her the following while fleeing: “Unfortunate Psyche, because I have fallen in love with you, I have gone against my mother’s wishes. You too went against my wishes. We can never be together again.” Psyche shed warm tears: how could she be forgiven by her lover?
Revenge
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
Psyche now understood the manipulation of her sisters and she decided to get revenge. Claiming ignorance, she went to see the eldest and told her her story. She changed just the end of the story: “now, Eros the god is looking for a new spouse. To enter in the palace, all one had to do was to throw oneself from the rock she was offered to the monster.” Believing her, her sister ran towards the rock and plunged into the abyss. But Zephyrus was not there to catch her and she drowned at sea. To her second sister she told the same story which led to the same result. Her sister also disappeared in the waves.
Eros hurted
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
Eros went back to his mother. The oil had burned him badly and he fell into the bed with great pangs of pain. When Aphrodite saw her son so cruelly wounded, she became enraged: who dared seduce her son? Psyche, this mortal? Once again this princess that bothered her? She would quickly see what she was made of! When she saw her leave, Hera, the wife of Zeus tried to reason with her. It was useless! The rage of Aphrodite was without limits.
The temple of Hera
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
Psyche was wandering the earth. She needed to find the way to reconquer her lover. But how? She went to pray at the temple of Hera and the goddess appeared: “Alas”, she told her, “I cannot help you! Aphrodite is very upset with you and has every intension for revenge. I do not want to go against her wishes because if gods begin to quarrel, it is the whole order of the world that could be endangered.” With these words, Psyche felt herself faint: if the wife of the great Zeus, the goddess of conjugal fidelity and family could not help her, where could she find help?
The messenger of the gods
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
Aphrodite was not waiting. She went to find Hermes, the messenger of the gods, who like her son, also had wings. She gave him a message to give in all regions of the Earth: “Aphrodite demands that Psyche be captured and given to her for the most sumptuous of rewards: 7 kisses from the goddess of beauty, and the last on the mouth.” With a prize like this, who could resist?
Psyche surrenders
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
All the men on earth went on the hunt to find Psyche. Psyche, understanding that she could not escape her fate, decided to meet her fate head on. She went by herself to the temple of Aphrodite, her mother in law, to turn herself in. Without looking at her condition, one of the followers of the goddess grabbed Psyche by the hair and brought her to the goddess.
