The palace of the god Eros
The sacrifice
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
It is impossible to escape the will of the gods, as cruel as it may be. Therefore they brought Psyche to the prescribed location and they left her alone. While she was accompanied, she stayed courageous to not further afflict her family. But when she was alone, she could not help but cry: what had she done to perish this way? Suddenly she felt a strong invisible breath that surrounded her and lifted her into the skies. She fainted from fright.
Zéphyr
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
It was Zephyrus, the god of wind who brought Psyche unconscious to a hidden palace, away from the eyes of men. She dropped her on a great bed of silk and rose petals. When she woke, she didn’t know where she was. The great palace was empty and yet Psyche was never alone. When she wanted something, she just had to utter the words and the Zephyrus, the invisible wind, would bring those to her. Fruits, drinks, dresses, jewelry... everything at her disposal.
Don't look at me
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
At night, she would fall asleep in a bed of rose petals. In the complete darkness she could feel a presence. But was it not that of a monster: instead it was an absolutely charming young man. Psyche wanted to light up a lamp to see who this person was but her nocturnal visitor would not allow it. “do not seek to see me or find out who I am. If you respect this wish, we live happy, together and forever.”
Psyche pregnant
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
Days and weeks went by in this fashion. The day, Psyche was the absolute master of a great empty palace and the wind would bring her all that she desired. At night, her lover would rejoin her and they would sleep together. Soon, Psyche became pregnant. Her new state made her realize what she had left behind. While she was happy at night with her lover, she was bored during the day and she missed her family.
